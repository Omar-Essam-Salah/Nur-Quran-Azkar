## Recitation audio
- `verses.quran.com`, `audio.qurancdn.com`, `mirrors.quranicaudio.com` — per-ayah
  recitations with word timing (Qur'an Foundation).

  (e.g. Maher Al-Muaiqly, Yasser Al-Dossari).
